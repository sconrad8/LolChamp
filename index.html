Who We Are?

Just two guys, no different than any of you, who aspire to create the best possible League of Legends environment. We’re both sophomores in college, studying computer science. We’ve played many games, most being very entertaining, but League of Legends stole our hearts. We’ve been avid LoL players since season 3; we’ve been around for many changes; we’ve been high ranks and we’ve been low ranks; but through it all, we have had unparalleled fun. With our growing knowledge in computer science and our great appreciation for League of Legends we decided to create LoLChamp.

Why we created LoLChamp?

After thousands of hours of gameplay, seeing both spectrums of skill level, and simply talking to fellow Summoners, we felt there was a void that needed to be filled. Sure you can go to some websites to view basic stats such as minions killed, champions killed, assists, etc., but we craved more than just those fundamental values. We were hungry for in-depth statistics that provide a much better insight as to things such as: how well you play, what champions you are best with, and how much you improve.

What we offer?

LoLChamp is an alternative to websites such as LoLKing and OP.GG. What differentiates us is easy-to-grasp, in-depth charts, graphs, and statistics so that you can not only see how well you do in any given game, but you can track your overall gameplay and most importantly, track your improvements. Our primary goal is to give you pertinent information that will allow you to improve on your gameplay. This being said, if you have any suggestions, criticism, or comments, please do not hesitate to post in our feedback section.

Your fellow Summoners,
-Mister Sleezy
